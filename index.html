About

I'm a generalist at heart. I'd rather understand a whole system than one slice of it, so I follow a problem wherever it goes and do whatever it takes to get something up and working.

I have a background in software development, but I'm also interested in design, businesses and branding. I like to think of myself as a creative problem solver, and I enjoy working on projects that have a positive impact on people's lives.

When I'm not at a keyboard you'll probably find me out with my friends, in the gym, doing music, enjoying a good meal or on a hike in the mountains.
